How ordering food with Super works

1. Tell Super what you want

Message Super with a natural request like “reorder my usual Thai dinner” or “find groceries for the week under my usual budget.”

2. Super uses real apps

Instead of relying on fragile integrations, Super operates the same delivery websites and apps you use, step by step.

3. Computer-use cache kicks in

Super reuses a computer-use cache of prior logins, addresses, and preferences so repeat orders get faster and cheaper over time.

4. You confirm before purchase

Super prepares the order and asks for confirmation before anything is placed, keeping you in control.
